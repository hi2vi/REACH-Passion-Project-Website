Keyboard Shortcuts

Chrome Keyboard Shortcuts(for Windows)

    Ctrl-T: New Tab

    Ctrl-X: Cut and Copy

    Ctrl-C: Copy

    Ctrl-V: Copy and Paste

    Ctrl-K: Search Google/link

    Ctrl-E: Search Google

    Ctrl-L: Search bar

    Ctrl-F: Find

    Ctrl-G: Find

    Ctrl-H: Find And Replace/ Search history

    Ctrl-Shift-A: Look at open tabs and previously open tabs

    Ctrl-Shift-T: Open last closed tab

    Ctrl-Shift-W: Close entire window

    Ctrl-W: Close currently open

    Ctrl-1: Go to first tab

    Ctrl-2: Go to second tab

    Ctrl-3: Go to third tab

    Ctrl-4: Go to fourth tab

    Ctrl-5: Go to fifth tab

    Ctrl-6: Go to sixth tab

    Ctrl-7: Go to seventh tab

    Ctrl-8: Go to eighth tab

    Ctrl-9: Go to last tab open
